# EU Data Sovereignty Policy (GDPR Compliance)

## 1. Data Residency
*   All Personal Data of EU citizens must be stored and processed EXCLUSIVELY within the European Economic Area (EEA).
*   **Prohibited**: Transfer of identifiable data to "Third Countries" (including the USA) without Binding Corporate Rules (BCRs) or Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs).

## 2. Infrastructure Restrictions
*   Primary databases must be hosted in `eu-west-1` (Ireland) or `eu-central-1` (Frankfurt).
*   Processing jobs (Lambdas, EC2 instances) must carry tags restricting them to these regions.

## 3. Backups & Disaster Recovery
*   Backups constitute "storage" and are subject to the same residency rules stated in Section 1.
