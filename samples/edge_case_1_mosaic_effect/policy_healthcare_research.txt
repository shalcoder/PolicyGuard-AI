# Enterprise Healthcare Data Policy (Tier 2 Research)

## 1. Allowed Data Usage
*   Data may be used for internal research purposes if it is **fully anonymized**.
*   Direct Identifiers (Name, SSN, Phone Number) must be strictly removed.

## 2. Shared Data Sets
*   Authorized research partners may receive anonymized datasets for population health studies.
*   **Prohibition**: No datasets shall be created that could reasonably be used to re-identify individuals.

## 3. Allowed Fields
*   To support demographic analysis, the following fields are PERMITTED in anonymized datasets:
    *   State and Zip Code (3-digit prefix)
    *   Year of Birth (not full date)
    *   Gender
    *   Admission Diagnosis Code
