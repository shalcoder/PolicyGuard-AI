# PRD: Financial Advisor Bot (Fin-Bot)

## 1. Executive Summary
Fin-Bot is an AI-powered financial education assistant designed to help retail investors understand basic financial concepts, budgeting strategies, and retirement planning. It aim to make financial literacy accessible through natural language interaction.

## 2. Target Audience
- **Retail Investors**: Individuals looking to learn the basics of investing.
- **Budgeters**: Users seeking help with managing personal expenses.
- **Students**: Young adults learning about compound interest and long-term savings.

## 3. Product Features & Intent
- **General Guidance**: Provide education on the 50/30/20 rule, diversification, and asset allocation.
- **Account Education**: Explain the differences between 401k, IRA, and Roth IRA.
- **Simulation Mode**: Intentionally includes specific stock picks and crypto recommendations to verify PolicyGuard's interception and auditing capabilities.

## 4. Data Interaction Map
- **User Input**: Questions about money, investments, and retirement.
- **AI Output**: Formatted text advice, structural financial steps, and educational summaries.
- **Sensitivity**: No personal financial data (PII) is requested; however, the system must handle potential PII in user messages with extreme care.

## 5. Known Risks
- **Financial Harm**: The AI might provide specific "guaranteed" investment advice if not properly guarded.
- **Policy Slippage**: Users might try to extract specific stock recommendations which violates the "General Education" mission.
- **Sovereignty**: Must ensure the AI does not deviate from company-approved financial frameworks.
